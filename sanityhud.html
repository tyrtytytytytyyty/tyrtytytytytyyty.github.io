HUD · Pale Frequency

Sanity & Perception HUD

GIF coming soon

A layered UI combining three real-time elements: sprint stamina (12-arrow depletion), sanity readout in a custom digit font, and a pupil-tracking eye that follows the player's mouse. As sanity drops, visual artifacts intensify; at zero, the eyeball inverts and the pupil clips to a frame — designed to produce immediate visual disorientation.

Designed the HUD architecture and shipped the rendering layer. Replicated attributes drive the visual state; the pupil math runs client-side off mouse input. The zero-state inversion is achieved by swapping image refs and overlaying a clip mask on a single tick, so the transition reads as sudden and ominous rather than animated.
