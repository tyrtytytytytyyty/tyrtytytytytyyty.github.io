V0 Generator · Ramble2Script

Voice-to-Script Generator

Demo coming soon

The original Ramble2Script generator — a single-page app that takes a voice memo or block of rambling text and returns a polished script. Audio gets transcribed via Whisper; Llama 3.3 70B (via Groq) extracts the actual point and rewrites it into hook + outline + body format.

Designed the pipeline and shipped V0 as a self-hosted single-file demo. The interesting product call: the system doesn't try to preserve the rambler's exact phrasing. It surfaces the intent and rewrites it sharply — which is what most creators actually want.
