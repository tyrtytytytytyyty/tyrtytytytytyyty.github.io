A level-construction system that lets players assemble map geometry mid-game. Players pick prefab pieces from a palette, place them in 3D space with grid snapping and rotation, and the layout persists per-player via DataStore — saved layouts survive across sessions and game updates.

Architected and shipped the placement, snapping, and persistence layers. The trickier parts: real-time collision feedback during placement, and preview ghosts that disambiguate valid vs invalid positions clearly.
